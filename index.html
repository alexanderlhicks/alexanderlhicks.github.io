Alexander Hicks

I am a researcher in computer science with broad interests in the security, privacy, transparency, and decentralisation properties of systems.

My current affiliation is UCL, where I am finishing a PhD thesis on transparency enhancing technologies under the supervision of Steven Murdoch in the information security group. Over the course of my PhD, I have also produced research on decentralisation and cryptocurrencies.

Before joining UCL, I obtained a BSc in Theoretical Physics from QMUL and a MASt in Mathematics from the University of Cambridge.
